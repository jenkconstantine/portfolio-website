About

It's nice to meet you! I'm a US-based designer that is currently situated in Boston, USA. I was born and raised in England, where I began my career as a freelance graphic designer.

I'm a big fan of the creative arts, and spend an unhealthy amount of time trawling sites looking for inspiration from graphic artists, multimedia-UI designers, animators, and web developers.

I have a wide range of experience creating designs and edits in the consumer electronics, education, and business start-up industries, and have chosen to focus my career on User-Interface design, going forward.
